Our Work

Our company provides small businesses with chickens at a low price when ordered in bulk. This also creates Job opportunities for unemployed people that are interested in farming.Our company owns a single farm at this moment but we are able to deliver exceptional good quality meat.

Our work is to provide clients with good meat as we are the chicken supplier for many small business with our low prices making them to save money and time by also offering delivery services if the client orders online and is unable to come and collect the stock/purchased goods.Our job is mainly focused on farming.


Our company offers a delivery option to help clients that are far away and need their purchased goods right away. This process makes everything to be easy because most clients will not come to the farm trying to buy their goods there rather than ordering online to make everything to be less complicated.

We always aim to sell our clients the healthiest chickens available to reduce diseases and etc.
